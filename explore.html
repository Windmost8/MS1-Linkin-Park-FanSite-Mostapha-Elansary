Who are they?
An Iconic Band!

This is Linkin Park, an American rock band famous for it's many hit songs across the years! Formed first in 1996 they would go on to change the face of Nu-Metal forever, and sell over 100 million records world wide, winning the adoration of fans along the way.

Unfortunately, one of the lead singers of Linkin Park, pictured below, has passed away, and thus the future of the band's music-making potential remains unclear. However this has not stopped the adoration of fans all around the globe, Linkin Park's music lives on, and as long as fans exist, so will their songs alongside them.

From left to right; Dave Farrel, Brad Delson, Rob Bourdon, Mike Shinoda, Joseph Hahn, and Chester Benington.
